Math Tank

MathTank is an online problem solving and development platform for teachers of children in grades K-8 looking to assign highly customizable problems to students.

Live Demo - Under Development

Required Features

  • UI/UX Optimization
  • Behavuor tracking and analysis
  • Ability to reference other tasks in assignment of a task
  • Complex symbols and operator keyboard
  • Ability to graph
  • Ability to assign "scratch paper" or a calculator
  • Word problem analysis and NLP support
  • Many More!!!!!
